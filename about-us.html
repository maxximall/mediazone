About Us

Our team supports all aspects of mental health and psychological risk management for production teams, cast, talent, and contributors across the lifespan of a project, for linear, streaming, and digital first content, TV, film, podcast, social media and performing arts.

Our Team

Our team of Clinical Psychologists collectively have over 50 years of experience in the TV and Film industry. Whilst productions may employ a Media Psychologist / TV Psychologist, this title is a generic self-assigned title and does not guarantee any degree of training or professional accreditation or proficiency. Our team are all Chartered Clinical Psychologists with British Psychological Society (BPS), registered with the Health & Care Professionals Council (HCPC) as Practitioner Psychologists (a protected title), with Practitioner Doctorates in Clinical Psychology (DClinPsych). This offers an assurance of a high standard of training and qualification, competence and accreditation.
