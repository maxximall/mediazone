About us

Mindzone Media specialises in supporting film, TV and media production companies, in the key areas of mental health and psychological risk management for production teams, cast and talent, and contributors.

Many production companies depend upon Mindzone Media’s training, consultations, assessments and reports to understand risks that may emerge in any given production, and guidance on support to contain and minimise risk.  We are aware of tight production schedules and demands, and can support flexible and reliable on-call, standby, and follow-up requirements.

The team of Consultant Clinical Psychologists have an impressive breadth of knowledge and experience working with large and small productions in the UK and Overseas.  Our consultation and services are tailored for the specific needs of each unique production, supporting the life span of a production from project development through to post-transmission aftercare
